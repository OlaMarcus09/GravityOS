# Gravity OS — Revenue Engine

## Free Plan
- Personal workspace
- One active project
- Basic planner
- Calendar
- Limited catalogue
- Basic reminders

Makes it easy for creators to start.

## Pro Plan
Monthly or annual subscription with:
- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited catalogue
- Release planner
- Marketing planner
- Budget planner
- Team collaboration
- Advanced analytics
- AI Manager
- Priority support

This becomes the primary recurring revenue line.

## Team Plan
Designed for: artist managers, small labels, creative agencies, studios.

Includes:
- Multiple workspaces
- Team permissions
- Shared calendars
- Approval workflows
- Organization dashboard

## Marketplace Revenue
Once enough creators are using Gravity OS, connect buyers and sellers.

Examples:
- A producer needs artwork → Gravity OS recommends vetted designers.
- An artist needs mixing → Gravity OS recommends engineers.
- A filmmaker needs a soundtrack → Gravity OS recommends producers.

Revenue comes from:
- Service commissions
- Featured listings
- Verified professional profiles
- Premium portfolios

## Brand Partnerships
Brands often want access to creators. Gravity can connect creators with:
- Campaigns
- Sponsored content
- Ambassador programs

## AI Subscription
Instead of a generic chatbot, it would:
- Build release plans
- Analyze catalogue health
- Recommend marketing actions
- Suggest budget allocations
- Identify collaboration opportunities
- Monitor consistency
- Generate weekly executive summaries
